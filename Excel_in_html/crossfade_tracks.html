10 10 "Constant Gain" ensures that, as long as the original audio is not clipped, the crossfade will not clip - this is the default. However, "Constant Gain" crossfades may cause the overall volume to dip slightly during the fade. "कॉन्स्टन्ट गेन" हे सुनिश्चित करते की, जोपर्यंत मूळ ध्वनी क्लिप केला जात नाही तोपर्यंत क्रॉसफेड ​​क्लिप करणार नाही - हे पूर्वनिर्धारित आहे. तथापि, "कॉन्स्टन्ट गेन" क्रॉसफेड्स मुळे फेड दरम्यान एकूण व्हॉल्यूम किंचित बुडेल.
11 11 Constant Power 1 सतत शक्ती 1
12 12 "Constant Power" fades combat the tendency of the volume dipping, so will often be the preferred choice, though care needs to be taken when using this type of crossfade as the peak level may rise during the course of the fade. "कॉन्स्टन्ट पॉवर" फेड्स व्हॉल्यूम बुडण्याच्या प्रवृत्तीचा सामना करते, म्हणूनच बहुतेकदा प्राधान्य दिले जाते, परंतु या प्रकारच्या क्रॉसफेड ​​वापरताना काळजी घेणे आवश्यक आहे कारण फेडच्या दरम्यान उच्च पातळी वाढू शकते.
13 13 "Constant Power 1" is generally a good choice as a general purpose crossfade effect. सामान्य प्रयोजन क्रॉसफेड ​​प्रभाव म्हणून "कॉन्स्टन्ट पॉवर 1" सामान्यतः चांगली निवड असते.
14 14 Constant Power 2 सतत शक्ती 2
1५ 1५ As with "Constant Power 1", this option is also designed to maintain a fairly constant volume through the course of the fade. This variation of the constant power fade starts to fade in a little more rapidly than "Constant Power 1" and the crossfading may sound a bit abrupt in the first or last moments of the fade, though it may be preferred when crossfading songs that are beat-matched. This version is based on the legacy "Cross Fade In" and "Cross Fade Out" effects of previous versions of Audacity. 1६ 1६ Custom Curve सानुकूल वक्र
1७ 1७ The preset options will cater for most crossfading tasks, but if fine control over the effect is required, the custom curve option may be useful. 4५ 4५ Optional extra steps पर्यायी अतिरिक्त चरणे
4६ 4६ The two tracks will automatically be mixed to one file on export, but if preferred, the tracks may be mixed down to one track within the project by applying Mix and Render from the Tracks menu. Mixing down to a single track can be useful when using "constant power" or custom curves, as it then allows the mix to be Normalized before export. निर्यातीवरील दोन गीतपट्टा स्वयंचलितपणे एका धारिकामध्ये मिसळले जातील, परंतु प्राधान्य दिल्यास गीतपट्टा मेनूमधून मिक्स आणि रेंडर लागू करून प्रकल्पात गीतपट्टा खाली एका गीतपट्टामध्ये मिसळले जाऊ शकतात.
